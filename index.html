MBAn Research Study

Designing for Attention in Digital Workspaces

A short research study on how interface design affects focus, distraction, and task performance during realistic workplace investigation tasks.

Thank you for taking a few minutes to support my research. Your participation helps me understand how people work through information-heavy digital tasks, and how better interface design may support attention and flow.

Study Timeline & Requirements

Please complete the entire study in a single sitting without refreshing or closing your browser. Answer based strictly on the information shown on your screen.

Step 1
3 Investigation Tasks
Time Limit
3m 30s per task
Step 2
1 Brief Questionnaire

Your Task Instructions

You will navigate a simulated digital operations workspace and complete short investigation tasks. Please ensure you follow these rules:

  1. Review the data presented in each scenario thoroughly.
  2. Submit answers to all questions on the screen before moving to the next task.
  3. Complete the final experience survey immediately following Task 3.

Data Privacy & Research Use

To assess behavioral interface changes, this application logs task answers, response timing, scrolling speed, page navigation, and interaction patterns. All data remains anonymous and is utilized exclusively for academic research analysis.

Clicking "Start Study" will randomly allocate your session to an interface test group and route you directly to Task 1.
