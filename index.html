MBAn Research Study

Designing for Attention in Digital Workspaces

A short research study on how interface design affects focus, distraction, and task performance during realistic workplace investigation tasks.

Thank you for taking a few minutes to support my research. Your participation helps me understand how people work through information-heavy digital tasks, and how better interface design may support attention and flow.

What you will do

You will complete three short investigation tasks in a simulated digital operations workspace. After the tasks, you will answer a short questionnaire about your experience.

  • Read the information shown in each task carefully.
  • Answer the task questions based only on what is shown on screen.
  • Complete the final questionnaire after Task 3.

Data collected

The study records your task answers, timing, scrolling, navigation, and interaction behaviour. This data will be used only for academic analysis.

When you click Start Study, you will be randomly assigned to one version of the workspace and taken to Task 1.
